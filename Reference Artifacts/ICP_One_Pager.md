# **IDEAL CLIENT PROFILE**

**STR Expansion Investor**

Independent short-term rental investors and small portfolio operators managing 5–20 units across 3+ U.S. markets, especially in regulated cities where permit rules, occupancy limits, taxes, and local enforcement can change quickly.

**Why this ICP works:** They experience recurring compliance pain during acquisition, expansion, renewal, and ongoing monitoring, while still being small enough to move quickly and large enough to pay for a focused compliance product.

## **Firmographics**

* Owner-operators or very small investment teams.  
* 5–20 STR units.  
* Operate in 3+ markets.  
* Buy or underwrite in regulated cities.  
* Usually self-manage compliance or rely on a lightweight team, VA, or property manager.

## **Behavioral Signals**

* Checks city/county STR laws before making offers.  
* Regularly compares markets for new acquisition opportunities.  
* Uses spreadsheets, city websites, attorneys, Reddit, or property managers to confirm rules.  
* Has already paid for legal or compliance help at least once.  
* Keeps an eye on ordinance changes, permit requirements, and renewal deadlines.

## **Trigger Events**

* Evaluating a new property or market.  
* Expanding into a new jurisdiction.  
* Permit renewal is coming due.  
* A city ordinance changes.  
* A nearby citation or complaint suggests rules may be tightening.  
* A deal is at risk because of STR restrictions.

## **Buying Motivation**

* Move faster on underwriting.  
* Avoid buying properties that cannot legally operate.  
* Reduce compliance mistakes and citations.  
* Avoid repeated attorney spend for every market check.  
* Create a repeatable diligence workflow across markets and properties.

## **Why They Buy**

* The pain is recurring, not one-time.  
* A single bad acquisition or compliance mistake can cost far more than a subscription.  
* They need fast, trustworthy answers at the property level.  
* The product can become part of their buying and operating workflow.
